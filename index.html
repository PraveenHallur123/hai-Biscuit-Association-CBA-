Our Mission

The Chai Biscuit Association (CBA) is dedicated to upholding the highest standards of chai and biscuit enjoyment. We foster unity, promote responsible dunking, and ensure that every member receives their fair share of cream biscuits. Our commitment to excellence is rivaled only by our commitment to never letting a biscuit drown in chai.

"For a stronger nation, one dunk at a time."
