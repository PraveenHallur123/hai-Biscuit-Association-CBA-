About the Chai Biscuit Association

Founded in 2021, the Chai Biscuit Association (CBA) was born out of a national emergency: a biscuit shortage during peak chai hours. Our founders, united by a love for crispy snacks and hot beverages, set forth to create an organization that would never let a biscuit go soggy before its time.

Today, CBA stands as the leading authority on chai-biscuit relations, setting standards for dunking, sipping, and polite biscuit sharing. Our annual "Dunkathon" is a highlight, and our research division is currently working on the world's first anti-soggy biscuit coating.

"We take our biscuits seriously, so you don't have to."
