Privacy Policy

The Chai Biscuit Association (CBA) is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect only the information necessary to ensure fair biscuit distribution and proper dunking etiquette. Your data will never be sold, traded, or used to determine your favorite biscuit (unless you tell us).

Information We Collect:
- Name, email, and favorite chai flavor (for contact purposes)
- Voting data (for election integrity)
- Biscuit preferences (for research, not judgment)

How We Use Your Information:
- To respond to your inquiries
- To improve biscuit-related services
- To ensure no member receives more than their fair share of biscuits

Security:
All data is stored securely in a biscuit tin (metaphorically speaking). Only authorized dunkers may access your information.

Contact:
For privacy concerns, please contact us via the form on our Contact page.

The Chai Biscuit Association is dedicated to the noble pursuit of dunking, sipping, and biscuit diplomacy. Join us for a better, crunchier tomorrow!
