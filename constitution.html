CBA Constitution

  1. No biscuit dunk shall exceed 3 seconds. (Unless it's a ginger snap, then all bets are off.)
  2. Members must respect all chai flavors, even "experimental" ones.
  3. Sharing biscuits is encouraged, but hoarding is a punishable offense.
  4. Tea must be served at a temperature suitable for immediate dunking.
  5. All meetings shall begin with a ceremonial dunk.
  6. Any biscuit found floating unattended shall be rescued promptly.
  7. Members may not discriminate against non-biscuit snacks, but must maintain biscuit superiority.
  8. Annual "Dunkathon" attendance is mandatory, unless excused by soggy biscuit syndrome.
  9. All official documents must be signed with a biscuit crumb.
  10. Fun is mandatory. Seriousness is optional.

About CBA

The Chai Biscuit Association is dedicated to the noble pursuit of dunking, sipping, and biscuit diplomacy. Join us for a better, crunchier tomorrow!
